Nothern Ireland is a country in the North-East region of the island Ireland. It includes Rathlin Island in the North Channel and several smaller offshore islands. Northern Ireland shares a 303 mile (488km) border with the Irish Republic. However unlike the Republic of Ireland, Northern Ireland is a part of the United Kingdom along with England, Scotland and Wales. Northern Ireland is the smallest country of these four.

Northern Ireland is made up of six counties - Fermanagh, Antrim, Tyrone, Derry/Londonderry, Armagh and Down. All homes to beautiful scenery and exciting attractions. Belfast is the heart and capital city of Northern Ireland and is full the concerts, and other nightlife and entertainment.

The largest freshwater lake in the British Isles can be found at the centre of Northern Ireland. This is Lough Neagh with 151 square miles. It is found where counties Antrim, Tyrone, Derry/Londonderry, Armagh and Down meet and provides 40% of the region's drinking supply.

Northern Ireland take part in a wide range of sports including:

  • Football
  • Rugby
  • Golf
  • Boxing
  • Gaelic
  • Cricket

Boxing

Boxing is a popular sport in Northern Ireland. Prominent Northern Irish boxers include Carl Frampton, Wayne McCullough and Ray Close.

Golf

Golf is a very popular sport in Northern Ireland. Between 2010 and 2015 Graeme McDowell, Darren Clarke and Rory McIlroy won 25% of the titles in Major Championships.

Football

The Irish Football Association (IFA) is the organising body for football (soccer) in Northern Ireland, and was historically the governing body for the whole of the island.

The Northern Ireland Women's Football Association (NIWFA) is the IFA's women's football arm. It runs a Women's Cup, Women's League and the Northern Ireland women's national football team.

The domestic league is the IFA Premiership. Some of the major teams include Linfield F.C., and Glentoran F.C.. Derry City FC is based in Northern Ireland but plays in the Republic of Ireland's league.

Northern Ireland also have an international team mainly made up of players from English and Scottish leagues with occasional appearances from Irish League players.

Rugby

Rugby union is a popular team sport played in Ireland. The sport is organised on an all-Ireland basis with one team, governing body and league for both the Republic of Ireland and Northern Ireland.

Gaelic

Gaelic games include Gaelic football, hurling, and Gaelic handball. >Gaelic games in Northern Ireland are controlled by the Ulster Provincial Board, which covers all nine counties of Ulster.

Every footballer or hurler plays for a local club, with the best players being selected for county panels. Each county has its own county championship, with the winners going on to play in the Ulster Senior Club Football Championship or Ulster Senior Club Hurling Championship.

Ice Hockey

The Belfast Giants have competed in the Elite Ice Hockey League since the 2000-01 season and are the sole Northern Irish team in the league. The team's roster has featured Northern Irish born players such as Mark Morrison, Graeme Walton and Gareth Roberts among others.
